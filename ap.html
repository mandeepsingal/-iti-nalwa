Note: The post of APO is vacant, and the Vice Principal of the ITI will exclusively look after the functions, role, and responsibilities of the TCPC Cell. The Principal, who was designated as Assistant Apprenticeship Advisor under the Apprenticeship Act, will not make any field visits for the purpose of apprenticeship. The member-secretary of the TCPC Cell will discharge all functions under the supervision of the Chairman.

Functions of TCPC:

  • Organize campus interviews for recruitment of trainees.
  • Maintain details of all trainees.
  • Collect the recruitment of skilled workforce for industries.
  • Provide counselling and career guidance to trainees by professionals and experts.
  • Conduct skill gap mapping and provide skill gap training to trainees.
  • Keep track of the passed-out trainees until they are employed for at least 3 years.
  • Conduct surveys in industries for apprenticeship training and to find out employment potential.
  • Act as an employment exchange.
  • Publicity center of excellence scheme.
