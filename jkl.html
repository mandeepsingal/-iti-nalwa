ITI Trades and Details

Duration: 2 years

Description: Learn to install, repair, and maintain electrical systems in homes and industries.

  • Wiring systems
  • Electrical machines
  • Safety protocols

Duration: 2 years

Description: Learn to assemble, install, and repair mechanical parts and systems.

  • Reading technical drawings
  • Welding and fabrication
