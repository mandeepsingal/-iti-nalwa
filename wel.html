ITI Welfare Programs

Support and Benefits for Students and Staff

Introduction to ITI Welfare

The ITI Welfare Program is designed to support the students and staff members by providing various benefits and resources that ensure their well-being, growth, and success both during and after their time at the ITI. The welfare initiatives are aimed at making a positive impact on the learning environment and providing opportunities for personal and professional development.

Welfare Programs Offered

  • Scholarships: Financial assistance is provided to deserving students to support their education and training programs.
  • Health & Safety: ITI ensures a safe and healthy environment for both students and staff with regular health check-ups and safety training sessions.
  • Counseling Services: Professional counselors are available for students to address academic and personal issues, guiding them to stay motivated and focused on their goals.
  • Job Placement Assistance: The welfare programs include job placement support, where students receive help in securing apprenticeships or full-time employment after completing their courses.
  • Financial Aid: Students facing financial difficulties may apply for emergency financial aid or assistance with training materials and other educational expenses.
  • Sports & Recreation: ITI organizes sports events and recreational activities to promote physical health and teamwork among students and staff.

Staff Welfare

Our ITI welfare program extends beyond students and includes benefits and initiatives for staff members as well. Some of the key benefits for staff members include:

  • Health Insurance: Comprehensive health insurance coverage for teaching and non-teaching staff.
  • Professional Development: Staff members are encouraged to attend workshops, seminars, and training sessions to enhance their skills and knowledge.
  • Retirement Benefits: Attractive retirement benefits and pension schemes to ensure a secure future for our staff members.
  • Leave Benefits: Generous leave policies that allow staff to take necessary breaks and attend to personal matters.
